Hello, I designed this website to show a little bit about my passion: cities and maps! I like watching maps, collecting maps, and making maps! For work or fun it's always fun. And of course I love playing the founder, planner, designer, and mayor in whatever city-planner video game is around.
I love looking at city maps and explore their intricate street layouts, see what's here and there, what's missing, figure out the "whys" behind their many urban forms. I love urban planning, maps, and playing with data. And perhaps a bit obsessed with putting all those things together in GIS. Since I came across with GIS my passion for cities took a different perspective. I was, and still, amazed by the potential combining spatial and statistical data not only for producing pretty maps, but also for revealing aspects of the city form that may otherwise remain undiscovered. After my first GIS course I started making my own maps and spatial analyses and putting them everywhere; as Ken Jennings, puts it in regards to books, no matter the subject, a paper or report will always look better with a map.
Currently, I am learning a few things that I missed out during school such as HTML/CSS, Leaflet, SQL and Python scripting.
